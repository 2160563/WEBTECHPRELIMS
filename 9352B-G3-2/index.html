Advocacies


Livelihood Programs

Livelihood seminars are held quarterly to impart knowledge to interested community members about handicrafting, massaging and other livelihood programs. the people in charge of the seminars train the participants to be skilled in those programs. given those skills, the people in that barangay can choose to apply the skills they have learned earn money.



Medical Mission

The Nazarene church and the barangay officials work together to be able to establish medical missions to help people in the barangay who are sick. they also stroll around the barangay to help and advise persons with disability to show their support for the people who needs more help than "others."

PWD Rights


Persons with disability(PWD) posses rights that are mandated by the government. These rights exist to cater their special needs may it be financial, architectural, and others, and to protect them from the possible threats of society. Such as privileges like discounts, priorities, etc.

About Barangay Puguis


Barangay Puguis

Puguis is the 3rd largest barangay of the Municipality of La Trinidad, Benguet. It is located on the southwestern part of the municipality, bounded on the north by barangay Wangal, on the south by Baguio City and the Municipality of Tuba, on the east by Barangay Betag and Pico and on the west by the Municipality of Sablan.

Mission

To unite the people within the Barangay and promote active participation towards achieving our vision. To exercise the Council’s maximum taxing powers granted by the Local Code. To partner experts in the conduct of programs and activities benefiting the whole community.

Vision

We envision Barangay Puguis a balanced, self-sustaining agricultural, and well-developed community with God-fearing, healthy and united people living within.
