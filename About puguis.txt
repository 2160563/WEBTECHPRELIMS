Barangay Puguis is the 3rd largest barangay of the Municipality of La Trinidad, Benguet.
It is located on the southwestern part of the municipality, bounded on the north by barangay
Wangal, on the south by Baguio City and the Municipality of Tuba, on the east by Barangay
Betag and Pico and on the west by the Municipality of Sablan.
